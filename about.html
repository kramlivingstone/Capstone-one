About Me

An aspiring Web Developer from Manila, where I am currently a student at Tuitt on their Coding Bootcamp that covers Full Stack Web Development. Formerly an IT Support Engineer at Sportadar, I shifted into programming. I graduated at STI College - Makati where I took up a Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology.
